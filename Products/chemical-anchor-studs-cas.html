Chemical Anchor Studs – CAS

Steel Zinc Plated | Stainless Steel A4-316

High-Performance Chisel-Ended Chemical Anchors for Concrete & Masonry

Description

The Chemical Anchor CAS is a chisel-ended anchor for use with any of our injection systems and glass capsules in masonry and concrete. On installation the stud becomes chemically bonded to the base material. The nut may be removed and replaced without affecting the anchorage.

Supplied complete with high-tensile hex nut, plain washer, and one external hex drive adapter per box.

Features & Benefits

  • • Suitable for non-cracked concrete
  • • High loading capacity
  • • Stainless Steel A4-316 for corrosive environments
  • • Works in dry & water-saturated concrete
  • • Custom lengths >1 meter available on request

Applications & Advantages

Ideal for structural fastening, machine foundations, railings, heavy steel structures, etc.
