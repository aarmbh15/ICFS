Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a chemical anchor and how does it work? +

A chemical anchor is a robust fastening solution designed to secure steel rods, bolts, or rebars into concrete or masonry using a resin-based adhesive system...

2. What are the advantages of chemical anchors over mechanical anchors? +

Chemical anchors provide higher load capacity, no expansion stress, vibration resistance, and work in damp or underwater conditions...

3. Can chemical anchors be used in cracked concrete? +

Yes, our premium chemical mortars are ETA-approved for use in cracked and non-cracked concrete...

4. What is the curing time for chemical anchors? +

Curing time varies from 20 minutes to 24 hours depending on temperature and resin type...
